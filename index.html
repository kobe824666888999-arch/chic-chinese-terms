地道中文 · Sound Chart

Tap the green native button to hear a real recorded voice. Tap a numbered tone button to hear that specific tone (device voice). The small stroke above each tone number shows its pitch shape.

Audio source: the green "native" buttons play a real recorded voice (one natural reading per syllable, not scoped to a specific tone — use it as a general reference for the sound). The four numbered tone buttons use your device's Chinese text-to-speech voice to demonstrate that exact tone, since separate real recordings per tone aren't available yet. The English approximations are rough guides too; several pinyin sounds (like x, q, ü) have no true English equivalent.
